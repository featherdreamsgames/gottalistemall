How to Play

  • List as many Pokémon as you can.
  • English names only.
  • You have one minute.
  • Each successful entry gives you 6 additional seconds.
  • Valid entries:
    • Species (Pikachu, Bulbasaur, Charizard)
    • Variants (Alolan Vulpix, East Sea Gastrodon)
    • Transformations (Mega Lucario, Primal Groudon)
    • Formes (Zen Mode Darmanitan, Sunshine Form Cherrim)
    • Mainline and Legends games only.
    • Exact spelling.
    • Try whatever you can think of. You might find some surprises.
    • Up to date as of the Winds and Waves teaser.
  • Press up to retrieve your last entry.
  • If you think a Pokémon is missing, please submit feedback.
